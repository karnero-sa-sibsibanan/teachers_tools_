This developer tool was engineered to match the exact mathematical guidelines, item counts, and manual calculation structures found in official early childhood manuals, providing public educators with a streamlined workflow helper to minimize manual computation errors.

Independent Kindergarten Assessment Helper Tools:

  • ECCD Checklist Child's Record Form 2 Data Assistant: An unofficial digital data matrix designed to help teachers track baseline computation, monitoring logs, and summary profiles for children aged 3 to 5.11 years old according to standard manual guidelines.
  • Automated e-ECD Checklist Calculation Helper: Unofficial entry templates mapping tracking parameters across seasonal reporting periods: Beginning of School Year (BoSY), Middle of School Year (MoSY), and End of School Year (EoSY).
  • ECCD Scaled Score Translation Utility: Built-in conversion math to help educators manually verify standard developmental interpretations from raw tally markers quickly.
  • Kindergarten Progress Tracking Sheet Interface: An independent progress summary configuration optimized to follow teacher grading submission guidelines cleanly.

Mathematical Coverage Mapped to the 7 Developmental Domains:

Our calculation utility features formula fields built strictly around the domain distributions of standard diagnostic profiles:

  1. Gross Motor Domain: 13 specific movement tracking metrics.
  2. Fine Motor Domain: 11 items checking finger coordination and palmar grasp transitions.
  3. Self-Help Domain: 27 crucial items covering feeding sub-domains and independent toilet training logs.
  4. Receptive Language Domain: 5 comprehension parameters testing instruction following.
  5. Expressive Language Domain: 8 indicators tracking vocabulary scale and query construction.
  6. Cognitive Domain: 21 operational items analyzing matching logic and size sorting sequences.
  7. Social-Emotional Domain: 24 interaction metrics logging cultural gestures (po, opo, mano) and peer cooperation rules.
